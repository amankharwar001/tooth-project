Here's What You'll Discover In Your Free Invisalign Consultation...

Consultation: Our experienced orthodontic team will assess your teeth and discuss your smile goals during an initial consultation.

Customized Treatment Plan: Using advanced 3D imaging technology, a personalized treatment plan will be created, showcasing the step-by-step movement of your teeth.

Aligner Production: Once the treatment plan is approved, a series of aligners will be custom-fabricated specifically for you.

Wearing Your Aligners: You'll wear each set of aligners for about two weeks, 20-22 hours a day, gradually shifting your teeth into alignment.

Progress Checkups: Regular checkups will be scheduled to monitor your progress and provide new sets of aligners as needed.

Enjoy Your New Smile: After completing your treatment, you'll be left with a stunning, confident smile that you'll want to show off to the world!
